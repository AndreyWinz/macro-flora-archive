# macro-flora-archive
A curated collection of macro photography focusing on the intricate details of flowers, trees, and wild plants. Documenting botanical beauty through the lens of various mobile and compact camera systems, this repository serves as a personal learning journey and an open-source gallery of high-quality, close-up nature imagery.

# Macro Flora Archive

Welcome to my collection of botanical macro photography. This project is a dedicated space for documenting the fine details of plants. From the veins in a leaf to the pollen on a stamen, or even just a cool photo of a flower, using accessible camera gear.

## Equipment
All photographs in this repository are captured using one of the following devices:
* **iPhone 14** (Current Primary)
* **iPhone 11 Pro**
* **Canon IXUS 220 HS**

## Project Status & Quality
I am currently a **learning photographer**. While these shots may not be professional-grade "top-notch" masterpieces yet, I am focused on improving my technique with every upload. 

### Inclusion Criteria:
To maintain the utility of the archive, all images must be:
1. **Macro-focused:** Subjects must be close-ups showing detail not easily seen from a distance.
2. **Clear:** No motion blur or missed focus on the primary subject.
3. **High Resolution:** Images must not be pixelated or heavily compressed.

## Repository Structure
The archive is organized by the scientific names of the flora:
`/[Scientific Name]/image_file.jpg`

*Example:*
`/Taraxacum Officinale/any_name.jpg`

## Contributions
Contributions are welcome! If you have high-quality macro shots of plants that fit the naming convention and quality standards mentioned above, feel free to open a Pull Request. Please ensure the folder name is the correct **Scientific Name** of the species.
